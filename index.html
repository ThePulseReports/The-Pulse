About The Pulse

Our Vision

To create journalism that feels alive. We want our words to inform, inspire, and spark conversation. The Pulse stands for truth, creativity, and community — a place where facts guide the story, not opinions.

We're a collective of students with different voices but the same purpose. We write, edit, design, and report because we care about stories that matter. Our work is grounded in accuracy, curiosity, and fairness.

The Pulse is student written and student led. We tell stories that move, question, and connect. Every piece reflects our generation and the world we're shaping — without agendas, bias, or politics. Just perspectives, facts, and honesty.
