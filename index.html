About The Pulse

The Pulse is a student-led news organization founded on one simple belief — knowledge should be accessible to everyone. We aim to bring clarity to complex issues, elevate youth perspectives, and build a platform that connects awareness with action.

We publish fact-based, balanced, and creative journalism across politics, finance, health, entertainment, science, and justice — with the goal of empowering informed readers and amplifying stories that make a difference.
