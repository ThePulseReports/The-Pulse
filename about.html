About The Pulse

The Pulse is a student-run news organization dedicated to delivering factual, balanced, and creative journalism. We believe knowledge should be accessible to everyone and aim to elevate youth perspectives while connecting awareness with action.

Our Mission

We strive to produce journalism that informs, inspires, and challenges. Our goal is not to push a political agenda but to empower readers with clarity, context, and compelling stories.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest journalistic standards.
  • Creativity: Presenting news in engaging and innovative ways.
  • Diversity: Amplifying multiple perspectives and voices.
  • Impact: Making information meaningful and actionable.

Why We Exist

The Pulse exists to bridge the gap between information and understanding. In a world overflowing with content, we aim to highlight what matters, spark dialogue, and nurture an informed community.
